About Louis C.K

Information:

Louis C.K or Louis Székely, is an american born actor and comedian. He was born in Washington, DC and moved to mexico city when he was one. He lived ther for most of his childhood. He left when he was 8 and moved to boston, where he grew up in his teenage years. This was when he found his passion for comedy. He spent his mid-teens in comedy clubs telling jokes. He found he was a very good comedian and he had a natural talent for making people laugh. The next 30 years were full of comedy specials, comedy tours, his own hbo show called "louis" and many different writing jobs for comedy. He has a special type of comedy, full of swears, vulgar humour and dark jokes. He takes his life and makes comedy out of it. How a normal person lives is his target and laughs.

His more famous works, being the comedy shows: "shameless", "oh my god", and "chewed up" is where you will find the bulk of his works. If you wish to see them, refer to "watch his shows".
